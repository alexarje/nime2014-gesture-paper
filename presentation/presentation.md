        Some core elements of most definitions

MacMillan:  

*a movement that communicates a feeling or instruction*     
*a. hand movement that you use to control some- thing such as a smartphone or tablet [...]*     
*b. the use of movement to communicate, espe- cially in dance*     


Three main categories:

- **Communication**: gestures are used to convey meaning in social interaction (linguistics, psychology)
- **Control**: gestures are used to interact with a computer- based system (HCI, computer music)
- **Metaphor**: gestures are used to project movement and sound (and other phenomena) to cultural topics (cog- nitive science, psychology, musicology)
